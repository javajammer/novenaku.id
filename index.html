About Us

Our Story & Mission

Who We Are

Novenaku is a Catholic prayer mobile application developed by a team of faithful Catholics who are passionate about making prayer accessible, beautiful, and deeply rooted in the traditions of the Church. Our name, "Novenaku," is inspired by the Catholic tradition of novenas — nine days of dedicated prayer and devotion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help Catholics around the world strengthen their faith through daily prayer, reflection, and devotion. We believe that a consistent prayer life is the foundation of a vibrant spiritual journey, and we are committed to providing the tools and resources that make daily prayer simple, meaningful, and deeply connected to Catholic tradition.

What We Believe

We believe in the power of prayer to transform lives. We believe that the Catholic tradition of novenas, the Holy Rosary, the Divine Mercy Chaplet, and daily devotions offer profound spiritual nourishment. We believe that technology, when used with reverence and purpose, can be a beautiful instrument for drawing closer to God.

Our Commitment

  • Faithfulness to Catholic teachings and traditions
  • Respect for the dignity of every person
  • Transparency and honesty in all we do
  • Providing a safe, family-friendly environment
  • Never making misleading spiritual claims or promises of miracles
  • Protecting the privacy and data of our users

Our Team

Novenaku is developed and maintained by a small, dedicated team of Catholic developers, designers, and theologians. We work closely with clergy and lay advisors to ensure that all content is accurate, reverent, and aligned with the teachings of the Catholic Church. We are not affiliated with any specific diocese or religious order, but we serve the universal Church with love and devotion.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use a mobile application. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our Services, and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

Novenaku uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic functionality of the App, such as remembering your session and preferences
  • Functional Cookies: Allow the App to remember choices you make, such as your preferred language, prayer settings, and notification preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the App so we can improve our Services. These cookies collect information in an aggregated and anonymized form

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device, including analytics providers (such as Google Analytics) and crash reporting services. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your device settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the App. Specifically:

  • iOS: Settings → Safari → Privacy & Security → Block All Cookies
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Advanced → Do Not Track
  • Browser: Most browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings menu

5. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, Novenaku may use local storage (such as localStorage or SQLite) to store your prayer progress, bookmarks, and offline content on your device. This data is stored locally and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync your data.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
